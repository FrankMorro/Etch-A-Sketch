Etch-A-Sketch

You can enter the amount of pixel you want to display on the canvas, and press Enter to update it. Note that changing this parameter resets the canvas. Allowable values are between 1 - 100. You can use the double click to activate the continuous line in any area of the canvas, and press click to deactivate it.
